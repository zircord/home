Zircord Closed Alpha

For its 100 million users, Discord provides no way to sift through its countless niche communities. Instead, newcomers rely on word-of-mouth or other social media to discover new servers.

Built on Go and Angular JS, we offer a natural language-driven search engine intent on fulfilling this need. Zircord uses topic modeling and keyword embeddings to match your server with similarly-minded users. We are currently in early testing stages, and are looking to give specific users early access.
